    private-detail DTO rows are present (they travel in the `e` detail array
    beside the typed slabs), so rich-delta DTO materialization is down to
    full/spawn rows plus a handful of private-detail rows. Remaining
    reduction targets, in current cost order at 512+ units: rich-full typed
    full applies (`clientApplyEntitiesTypedFull`, overlaps issue [30]),
    rich-delta generic entity apply spikes (`clientApplyEntitiesGeneric`,
    overlaps issue [30]), and the per-snapshot `visibility` stage (overlaps
    issue [28]). Projectile deltas
    should stay on metadata-only wire rows for despawns, velocity updates, and
    beam target rows; do not reintroduce packed beam DTO materialization on the
    client apply path. Server-side DTO materialization should stay bounded to
    full/spawn rows plus unavoidable private factory/turret rows; do not
    reintroduce command-state DTO fallbacks for unit builder/factory flags.
    Do not force empty heartbeat snapshots just to make the total SPS display
    read 30; non-empty entity/projectile deltas are event-driven. Use the
    received/applied cadence, bytes, materialization row/stage, and
    client-apply counters to verify each further reduction.

    force batch reads and writes canonical state directly.
