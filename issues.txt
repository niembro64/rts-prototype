[049][ARCH] src/game/sim/combat/projectileSystem.ts (~1800 lines) still owns
projectile tick orchestration around the WASM ProjectilePool: TS validates and
scatters packed pool slots, gathers non-packed homing/D-gun rows, updates
beam/laser lifecycle and tracing, and drives collision/despawn orchestration.
Move the remaining projectile tick state/result buffers into Rust so TS only
spawns/despawns/emits events, then delete the TS tick loops.

[051][ARCH] src/game/sim/combat/targetingInputStamping.ts (~975 lines) is the
TS->Rust stamping shim for the targeting FSM. Eliminate the per-tick
stamp/readback by making targeting state, snapshots, rendering, and downstream
combat consumers read the Rust slabs directly, then delete the shim.

[052][ARCH] src/game/sim/damage/DamageSystem.ts (~2355 lines) still
materializes damage resolution side effects in TS after Rust classification/HP
batching: knockback vectors, death contexts, killer bookkeeping, and
damage/death event assembly are still produced from TS entity objects. Move
those resolution outputs into Rust-owned event/result buffers so TS receives
typed events/knockbacks only, then delete the remaining TS resolution
math/helpers.
