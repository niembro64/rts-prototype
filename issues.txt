ISSUE: iOS WebKit crashes during startup after showing the loading screen twice.

Observed on iPhone Safari and iPhone Edge. Because all iOS browsers use WebKit,
the repeated loading screen likely means the WebKit page/GPU process crashes,
Safari auto-reloads the page, and then crashes again. Work through these
repo-side fixes one by one:

- Disable the 3D loading-unit preview on iOS/mobile so the loading overlay does
  not create an extra Three/WebGL renderer while the main battle renderer is
  starting.
- Skip or defer `renderer.compileAsync()` shader warmup on iOS/mobile; do not
  block startup readiness on mobile shader precompilation.
- Reduce mobile main-renderer pressure in `ThreeApp`: cap mobile pixel ratio at
  1 and disable antialiasing for mobile-like browsers.
- Reduce or bypass mobile PMREM/environment-map setup during startup if the
  crash remains after the loading preview and shader-warmup changes.
