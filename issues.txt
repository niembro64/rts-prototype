LOCOMOTION / UNIT BLUEPRINT UNIFICATION
================================================================================
Design decision (spec: budget_design_philosophy.html -> "Locomotion is part of
the unit, not a separate piece"): locomotion is NO LONGER a separate atomic
piece. A unit's movement profile and rig are authored INLINE on the unit
blueprint. There is no separate locomotion blueprint family and no separate
locomotion entity: locomotion mints no EntityId and carries no cost, no health,
no mass, and no lock-on eligibility of its own. It shares the unit body's
id/cost/health and explodes alongside the unit. Turrets cannot lock onto it.

The doc already reflects this; the CODE does not. The code still models
locomotion as a fully separate, addressable, costed, healthed entity (its base
ledger in locomotion.json today authors cost {energy:5, metal:5}, health, mass,
and a deathExplosion). Unify it. This is a large cross-language refactor:
locomotion is referenced ~58x in rts-sim-wasm/src/lib.rs and across ~80 TS
files. File:line refs below were correct when written; re-verify before editing.

L1. [LARGE] Fold the locomotion blueprint into the unit blueprint; delete the
    separate locomotion blueprint family.
    LOCATION:
      - src/game/sim/blueprints/locomotion.json (the whole family) and
        locomotion.ts (loader/validator); LocomotionBlueprint in types.ts.
      - src/game/sim/blueprints/units.json — every unit names its movement by
        "locomotionBlueprintId" (lines 52, 120, 195, 263, 331, ...). Inline the
        movement profile + rig config onto the unit itself instead.
      - Schema: src/game/sim/blueprints/blueprintSchema.json,
        src/types/blueprintSchema.generated.ts (LocomotionBlueprint* types), and
        rts-sim-wasm/src/generated_blueprint_schema.rs.
    GOAL: a unit blueprint owns its locomotion (type, physics, rig config,
    pathfinding) as its own fields. No locomotion.json, no getLocomotionBlueprint,
    no locomotionBlueprintId indirection. Movement is one of the unit's fields.

L2. [LARGE] Remove locomotion as a separate entity in the Rust sim; make it part
    of the unit body.
    LOCATION: rts-sim-wasm/src/lib.rs — ENTITY_META_KIND_LOCOMOTION (11685),
    ENTITY_META_BLUEPRINT_KIND_LOCOMOTION (11692), and the ~58 locomotion refs.
    GOAL: locomotion no longer mints its own EntityId, is not spawned or torn off
    as a child entity, and carries no separate health/cost/mass/deathExplosion.
    The unit body owns movement; killing the unit destroys its locomotion with it
    (it "explodes alongside the unit"). Absorb the per-locomotion base ledger
    (cost/mass/health/deathExplosion/radius from locomotion.json) into the unit
    body so a unit's cost and health are not split with a drivetrain sub-entity.
