object and scenegraph loops.

src/game/render3d/UnitDetailInstanceRenderer3D.ts

ClientViewState prepares body HUD, body-name, shield, contact-shadow,
ground-print, unit render-row, and building render-row packets. Unit/building
rows carry render keys, entity/turret row references, and dirty/lifecycle flags,
and the renderers consume those rows without steady-state per-row Entity map
lookups. Unit base-pose chain generation (tilt quaternion, inverse tilt,
parent quaternion, lifted position, and parent matrix) and unit chassis
part-matrix generation now run as batched WASM scratch-buffer kernels.
Instanced shield-panel slab matrices also run through a WASM scratch-buffer
kernel. The remaining hot work is still TypeScript pose generation and
Three.js scenegraph churn: turret pose, mirror support-root scenegraph work,
locomotion, fade, overlays, and cache pruning; plus per-building mesh, turret,
animation, overlay, and cache-pruning work. This is likely to dominate at the
"thousands of units" target.

Required fix:
Move as much remaining unit turret pose, mirror support-root work, building
pose, locomotion, fade, overlay, and cache-pruning work as practical into WASM
or GPU-updated buffers, then restrict Three.js work to explicit buffer range
uploads and lifecycle changes.
