  [HIGH] Player-name resolution has an orphan path. The active resolver
    is `resolvePlayerName` (GameCanvas.vue:292) + `getDefaultPlayerName`
    (playerNamesConfig.ts). But src/components/uiUtils.ts:97-99 still
    exports `getPlayerName(playerId)` — never imported. Delete it.

    localUsername (if local) → fallback. Merge into one function with

  [MEDIUM] Mirror panel-center math is computed in THREE sites with
    drift risk:
      - src/game/sim/combat/MirrorAimSolver.ts:175-204
        (uses resolveWeaponWorldMount + armLength * (cos α cos β,
        sin α cos β, sin β))
      - src/game/sim/combat/MirrorPanelHit.ts:94-100
        (uses unit-relative pivot from panel.offsetY + body-mid Z, then
        armLength * (fwdX cosPitch, fwdY cosPitch, sinPitch))
      - src/game/render3d/Debris3D.ts:816-851
        (uses chassis-yaw rotated armLength * cY/sY only — no pitch)
    Each computes pivot differently. Risk: future change to one
    formula breaks the others silently. Extract a single
    `getMirrorPanelCenter(unitX, unitY, unitGroundZ, panel, mirrorYaw,
    mirrorPitch)` helper next to mirrorPanelCache.ts.
