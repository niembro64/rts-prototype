CODE AUDIT - 2026-06-05

OPTIMIZATIONS TO MAKE

1. Unit/building render pose work still spends too much time in TypeScript
object and scenegraph loops.

Files:
src/game/network/ClientViewState.ts
src/game/render3d/EntityRenderPackets3D.ts
src/game/render3d/Render3DEntities.ts
src/game/render3d/BuildingEntityRenderer3D.ts

ClientViewState prepares body HUD, body-name, shield, contact-shadow,
ground-print, unit render-row, and building render-row packets. Unit/building
rows carry render keys, entity/turret row references, and dirty/lifecycle flags,
and the renderers consume those rows without steady-state per-row Entity map
lookups. The remaining hot work is still TypeScript pose generation and
Three.js scenegraph churn: unit group transforms, tilt, turret pose, shield
panel pose, locomotion, fade, overlays, and cache pruning; plus per-building
mesh, turret, animation, overlay, and cache-pruning work. This is likely to
dominate at the "thousands of units" target.

Required fix:
Move as much unit pose-matrix, turret pose, shield-panel pose, building pose,
locomotion, fade, overlay, and cache-pruning work as practical into WASM or
GPU-updated buffers, then restrict Three.js work to explicit buffer range
uploads and lifecycle changes.
